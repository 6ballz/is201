Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

    1. Ingredients
      • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
      • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
      • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
      • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
      • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
      • 1 teaspoon salt
      • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
      • 2 large eggs
      • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips
    2. Instructions
      1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (177 degrees Celsius).
      2. In a small bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda; set aside.
      3. In a large bowl, combine the butter with both sugars; beat on medium speed until light and fluffy.
      4. Reduce speed to low; add salt, vanilla, and eggs. Beat until well mixed, about 1 minute.
      5. Add dry ingredients; mix until just combined.
      6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
      7. Drop heaping tablespoon-size balls of dough about 2 inches apart on baking sheets lined with parchment paper.
      8. Bake until cookies are golden around the edges, but still soft in the center, about 12 to 15 minutes.
      9. Remove from oven, and let cool on baking sheet 1 to 2 minutes.
      10. Transfer to a wire rack, and let cool completely.
